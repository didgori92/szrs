About Us

GOA is an online academy in Georgia which is here to help out people of all ages in leaning programming in order to become more successfull in society and not have to worry about financial problems.
GOA was created by Nika Keshelava, who is the ceo of the academy. in GOA, all learning takes place online on discord, and you can choose how many lessons you may want per week, from 1 to 3 lessons a week.

Are there any meetups?

Great question, and yes there are! besides the monthly hackathons, there are plenty of other times where GOA members meet up for something, but, if you dont wish to go, that is entirely up to you.

What can i learn here?

A lot! you can learn Python, HTML, CSS, JS etc. and each lesson will teach you each one in detail for you to become a great programmer, just make sure to not be lazy and pay attention during lessons!
