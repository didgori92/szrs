What Is GOA?

That is a great question! and i am here to answer it!
Goal-Oriented Academy, or for short, GOA, is an online Academy in Georgia that focuses on helping people of all ages to learn programming and lead them to a better future!
Not only that, but it also helps you move away from bad habits, such as endlessly gaming or scrolling and wasting your time, by instead encouraging you to work on yourself and your knowledge!

Whats the learning like?

Im sure this question already popped up in your head, and the answer? it is great! not only is each lesson talked about in detail, but you can also always ask for help from your mentors! but understanding the problem yourself is always encouraged!

How can i attend classes?

Its simple! all you have to do is join the GOA discord server, through which you can attend your online lessons!

Would you reccomend GOA to people learning programming?

Yes! Absolutely! it is a wonderful and friendly place, and on top of learning todays most demanding profession, you also make new friends!
